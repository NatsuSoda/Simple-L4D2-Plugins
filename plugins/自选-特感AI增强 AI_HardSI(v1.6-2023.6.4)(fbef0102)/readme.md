## 配置指令 (ConVar)

- `ai_assault_reminder_interval` 默认值: `2`
- `AI_HardSI_Boomer_enable` 默认值: `1` - 0=Improves the Boomer behaviour off, 1=Improves the Boomer behaviour on.
- `AI_HardSI_Charger_enable` 默认值: `1` - 0=Improves the Charger behaviour off, 1=Improves the Charger behaviour on.
- `ai_charge_proximity` 默认值: `300` - How close a charger will approach before charging
- `ai_aim_offset_sensitivity_charger` 默认值: `20` - If the charger has a target, it will not straight pounce if the target's aim on the horizontal axis is within this radius
- `ai_health_threshold_charger` 默认值: `300` - Charger will charge if its health drops to this level
- `AI_HardSI_Hunter_enable` 默认值: `1` - 0=Improves the Hunter behaviour off, 1=Improves the Hunter behaviour on.
- `ai_fast_pounce_proximity` 默认值: `1000` - At what distance to start pouncing fast
- `ai_pounce_vertical_angle` 默认值: `7` - Vertical angle to which AI hunter pounces will be restricted
- `ai_pounce_angle_mean` 默认值: `10` - Mean angle produced by Gaussian RNG
- `ai_pounce_angle_std` 默认值: `20` - One standard deviation from mean as produced by Gaussian RNG
- `ai_straight_pounce_proximity` 默认值: `200` - Distance to nearest survivor at which hunter will consider pouncing straight
- `ai_aim_offset_sensitivity_hunter` 默认值: `30` - If the hunter has a target, it will not straight pounce if the target's aim on the horizontal axis is within this radius
- `ai_wall_detection_distance` 默认值: `-1` - How far in front of himself infected bot will check for a wall. Use '-1' to disable feature
- `AI_HardSI_Jockey_enable` 默认值: `1` - 0=Improves the Jockey behaviour off, 1=Improves the Jockey behaviour on.
- `ai_hop_activation_proximity` 默认值: `500` - How close a jockey will approach before it starts hopping
- `AI_HardSI_Smoker_enable` 默认值: `1` - 0=Improves the Smoker behaviour off, 1=Improves the Smoker behaviour on.
- `AI_HardSI_Spitter_enable` 默认值: `1` - 0=Improves the Spitter behaviour off, 1=Improves the Spitter behaviour on.
- `ai_spitter_bhop` 默认值: `1` - Flag to enable bhop facsimile on AI spitters
- `AI_HardSI_Tank_enable` 默认值: `1` - 0=Improves the Tank behaviour off, 1=Improves the Tank behaviour on.
- `ai_tank_bhop` 默认值: `1` - Flag to enable bhop facsimile on AI tanks
- `ai_tank_rock` 默认值: `1` - Flag to enable rocks on AI tanks
